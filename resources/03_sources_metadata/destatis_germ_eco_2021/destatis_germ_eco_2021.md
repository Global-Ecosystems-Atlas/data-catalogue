## Abstract
The ecosystem atlas (only in germany) is an online map viewer that presents the diversity and distribution of ecosystems in Germany. It includes maps of 74 ecosystem classes showing their distribution on municipality, district, regional and federal level for different years. In addition, the atlas provides overview raster maps (spatial resolution 100 metre), maps of selected ecosystems condition variables and a download function for geo-referenced data.
